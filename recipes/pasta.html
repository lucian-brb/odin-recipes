Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Description:

Creamy Alfredo Pasta is a simple yet delicious Italian dish made with tender pasta tossed in a rich and creamy Alfredo sauce. The sauce is typically made with butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese, creating a smooth and indulgent texture that pairs perfectly with the pasta. It's an easy meal that's both comforting and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 250g fettuccine or any pasta of choice
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer. Stir occasionally to avoid burning.
  4. Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
